# How to edit your website 🌿

You can change the words and photos on your website yourself — no technical
skills needed. Here's how.

## Signing in

1. Go to **www.munrocentre.com/studio**
2. Sign in with your email (the one you were invited with).

That's it — you're in the editor.

## What you'll see

On the left is a list of everything you can edit:

- **Homepage** — all the words and photos for each part of the home page
  (welcome message, about, meals, fees, and so on). It's split into tabs along
  the top (Hero, About, Community, Meals…).
- **Site settings** — your phone number, email, address and opening hours.
- **Rooms** — add, edit or reorder your rooms.
- **Educators** — add or update your team members.
- **Testimonials** — add real family reviews.
- **FAQs** — questions and answers.
- **Features / Trust bar** — the little highlight items.

## Changing text

1. Click the item you want to change (e.g. **Homepage**).
2. Click into any text box and type your changes.
3. Click **Publish** (bottom right). Your website updates within a minute.

## Changing or adding a photo

1. Click the image area.
2. Drag a photo in, or click **Upload**.
3. Add a short **alt text** (a few words describing the photo — helps Google and
   screen readers).
4. Click **Publish**.

> Tip: use clear, well-lit landscape photos where you can. The site
> automatically resizes and optimises them.

## Adding a new educator, room, review or FAQ

1. Click the type on the left (e.g. **Educators**).
2. Click **+ Create** (top).
3. Fill in the fields and **Publish**.
4. Use the **Order** number to control the position (lower numbers show first).

## Removing something

Open the item, click the **⋮** menu (top right) → **Delete**, then **Publish**.

## Good to know

- Nothing goes live until you click **Publish**. You can save drafts safely.
- Made a mistake? Open the item → **⋮** menu → **Review changes** to see history
  and restore an earlier version.
- If something looks broken, don't worry — call your web helper. You can't break
  the site's design from here, only its words and pictures.
